Biology Questions

What's an independent variable?

An independent variable is a variable that isn't changed by other variables.

What's a dependent variable?

A dependent variable is the variable being tested and measured in an experiment.

The building blocks, or monomers, of nucleic acids are...

They are nucleotides.

Are the terms "prokaryote" and "bacteria" the same thing?

Yes they are.

What are the building blocks of protein?

They are amino acids.

The characteristics of all living things include:

It includes being made of cells and having a metabolism.
